NKS CAs: Squaring (8-color) & Primes (16-color)

Implements the 8-color rule set from the NKS note “Rules for the squaring cellular automaton” with initial condition Append[Table[1, {n}], 3] padded by zeros. Each row is the next CA state (radius 1). Colors are arbitrary but consistent across rows. Source: NKS notes.

States: 0..7

Implements the 16-color prime-computing CA from the NKS note “Rules for the primes cellular automaton” with initial condition {10, 0, 4, 8} padded by zeros. This reproduces the construction that marks composites (gray stripes to the left in NKS) and leaves gaps at primes. Source: NKS notes.

States: 0..15
