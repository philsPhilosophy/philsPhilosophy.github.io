Rule 110 — gliders, ether, and collisions

Click on the canvas to toggle cells before running.
Demos seed ether + place movers at suggested spacing for a visible interaction.

Patterns used. Ether (period-14) 00010011011111. Right-mover core 0001110111. Left-mover core 1001111. Stationary core 111. These behave as gliders when embedded with correct phasing within the ether background and with suitable spacing; we provide simple presets so you can see them traveling and colliding. For a full catalog, see Martínez et al.; for universality via cyclic tag systems, see Cook (2004).
